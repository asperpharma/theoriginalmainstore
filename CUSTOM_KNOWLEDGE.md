# Asper Beauty Shop — Custom Knowledge & Project Mandates

## 💎 Project Core DNA: "Medical Luxury"
*   **Identity:** A fusion of high-end clinical authority (40%), luxury boutique experience (30%), and smart AI technology (30%).
*   **Visual Language:** "Asper Shine" — Ivory palette (#F8F8FF), gold accents, high-key clinical lighting, and white shimmering diagonal light beams on cards.
*   **Persona Lead:** Dr. Sami (Clinical/Science) and Ms. Zain (Aesthetic/Luxury).

## 🤖 AI Concierge: Dr. Bot
*   **Visual Face:** A friendly, high-fidelity mini-doctor character (`/dr-bot-character.png`).
*   **Role:** An interactive dermatological assistant that peeks from the bottom corner of the site. 
*   **Behavior:** Uses bilingual (Arabic/English) sales logic, upselling to a 3-Step Regimen (Cleanser, Treatment, Protection).
*   **Guardrail:** Always state: "I provide professional skincare guidance, not medical diagnosis."

## 🛠️ Coding & Design Conventions
*   **Typography:** Playfair Display (Headings), Montserrat (Body), Tajawal (Arabic).
*   **Layout:** Asymmetric, editorial Vogue-style grids. Use 10-second ease transitions for image scaling.
*   **Integrity:** 100% realistic brand imagery (Vichy, La Roche-Posay, etc.). No placeholders or dummy URLs.
*   **Security:** High-security posture for customer skin history. Strict Row Level Security (RLS) in Supabase.

## 🚀 Technical Stack
*   **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion.
*   **Backend:** Supabase (Auth, Postgres, Edge Functions).
*   **Commerce:** Shopify Storefront API.
*   **Brain:** Google Gemini (via Lovable Gateway).
